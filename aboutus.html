About Us

Our Proposal

For our prototype site we wanted it to have more consistency with bcit.ca's website and we also liked some aspects of the bcitcomp.ca website. We chose the colours schemes of bcit.ca's website and kept some of the layout components of bcitcomp’s website. The header and navigation bar is similar to bcitcomp.ca's website. We wanted the prototype site to be more readable and modern looking with colours and fonts. We also wanted to showcase what HTML5, jQuery can do like we used jQuery to make the lecture list more intuitive for students to navigate and read. Even though our prototype still has a long way to go to achieve what we ultimately want to bring to the student body, but we wholeheartedly believe that our website concept can bring a more consistent, more streamline, and more intuitive studying experience to BCIT’s students in the future, and showcase what us as alumni are capable of.
